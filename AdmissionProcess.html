Admission Process

Four-year program:

Capacity: 2 classes (total of 60 students from 9th grade of elementary school)

Admission criteria:

  • Results of entrance exams in Czech Language and Mathematics (CERMAT)
  • Academic performance in elementary school (core subjects: Czech Language, Foreign Language, Mathematics, History, Physics, Biology)
  • Bonus points for outstanding achievements in subject-related competitions

Eight-year program:

Capacity: 1 class (total of 30 students from 5th grade of elementary school)

Admission criteria:

  • Results of entrance exams in Czech Language and Mathematics (CERMAT)
  • Academic performance in elementary school (core subjects: Czech Language, Foreign Language, Mathematics, Science)
